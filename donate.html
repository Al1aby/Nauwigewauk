Why It Matters

Powered by Volunteers

🍁

The Nauwigewauk Community Club is a not-for-profit organization, incorporated in New Brunswick and entirely volunteer-run. It has served this community since 1948. Donations directly support the upkeep and growth of the grounds we all share.

Your contribution helps fund:

  • Maintenance of the picnic shelter and grounds
  • The nature trail network and signage
  • The playground, rink, ball diamond, and pump track
  • Community events throughout the year
Please note: While Nauwigewauk Community Club Inc. is a registered not-for-profit organization, we are not a registered charity, so we're unable to issue official tax receipts for donations at this time.

Donate by e-Transfer

  1. Open your online banking or banking app
  2. Choose "Send an Interac e-Transfer"
  3. Enter the amount and send to the email below
  4. If asked for a security question, please contact us first
Send e-Transfers To donations.ncc@gmail.com

Feel free to include a note in your transfer if you'd like your donation directed toward something specific — otherwise, it goes toward general club upkeep.
