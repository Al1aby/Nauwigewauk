What We Offer

Community Spaces

The Nauwigewauk Community Club maintains grounds and facilities for the benefit of the local community, located at 36 Darlings Island Road. Our picnic shelter and grounds are available for a range of events and everyday use.

🏕️
Picnic Shelter

A covered, open-air shelter on the grounds — a versatile space suitable for gatherings, celebrations, and community events of all sizes.

  • Picnic tables included
🛝
Community Playground

A playground on the grounds for children and families to enjoy, open to the community year-round.

  • Free and open to the public
  • Adjacent to the picnic shelter
Seasonal ⛸️
Outdoor Skating Rink

An outdoor rink open to the community during the winter months — a favourite local tradition.

  • Open in winter, weather permitting
  • Free and open to the public
  • Lit for night skating
🚲
Bicycle Pump Track

A pump track on the grounds for cyclists of all ages and skill levels to ride and practice.

  • Free and open to the public
  • Helmets recommended
⚾
Baseball Diamond

A full baseball diamond on the grounds, available for games, practices, and community use.

  • Free and open to the public
🏐
Multiuse Court

A multiuse sport court on the grounds, suited to a range of activities for all ages.

  • Free and open to the public
🌳
Grounds & Parking

Ample outdoor space at 36 Darlings Island Road, with on-site parking for guests and visitors.

  • Home to the picnic shelter, playground, rink, ball diamond, court, and pump track
  • Accessible entrance
🌿
Nature Trail

A network of six named trails through the natural landscape of the Nauwigewauk area, connecting to the historic covered bridge — open to all.

  • Free and open to the public
  • Year-round access
