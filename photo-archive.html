Share Your Photos

Do you have photographs of Nauwigewauk, the old hall, community events, or local history? We'd love to add them to the archive.

Submit a Photo
  • Digital scans or photos of old prints are both welcome
  • Please include the approximate year and any context you know
  • Photos of people, events, the old hall, the picnic shelter, the trail, and the landscape are all valued
  • Submitted photos may be added to the website and/or the club's records
