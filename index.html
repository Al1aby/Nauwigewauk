Welcome to Nauwigewauk

Where community
still gathers.

Shaped by the river. Rooted in volunteers. Open to everyone.

Nestled between Hampton and Quispamsis along the Hammond River, the Nauwigewauk Community Club has brought neighbours together since 1948 — from the picnic shelter and grounds to the nature trail beyond.

Come for an event, or stay for the community.
